# Journals

A simple application to store your jounrals with proper categorising of your own, and no limits to amount allowed.
You just need a google account to signin and start using the app, no more hustles.
No more paying for Journals
No more cutting trees to make paper for journals.

You can add, edit, remove notes from your Journal.
The UI is simple and clear, even a 1 year old can use it.

This App makes you a better person and the world a better place.
